Blender Add-on

Better Randomizer Panikradius

This Blender add-on makes the randomization of location, rotation and scaling of objects easier and more comfortable. The add-on is based on the Blender function "Randomize Transform" and adds these features:

- all parameters always accessible in the n-panel
- snapping option
- create duplicates of objects quickly
- one click keyframing for location/rotation/scale
- and more!

While Better Randomizer works with any object(s) in Blender, it was created with a workflow in mind where you use randomization as inspiration for finding interesting shapes. Starting with a set of kit bashing parts (some are included with the add-on), you can easily iterate through infinite combinations of objects.
